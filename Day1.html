5. what is the difference between block and inline

Block: the elements always begin with a new line and it extends from left to right. It takes the full width available on the screen. Browser automatically adds some space(margin) before and after the element. Ex:- div, h1 to h6, p, ol, ul, table, etc.
Inline: does not begin on a new line. It only takes space that is necessary for it. Ex:- span, img, input, a, label.
